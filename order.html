About Telugu Foods

Hey! We’re Telugu foods – as the names gives it away, we’re your one-stop-shop for anything authentically Telugu! That’s the thing we’re going for, but we’re also going for healthy, easy and fun! We opened in 1979, where we sold under the name of ‘Vamana Pickles’, but today we have _ locations and counting in USA, … and beyond! Since the beginning, we’ve gathered the best ingredients, spices, and time-honoured techniques of Indian cooking and brought them together for the most delicious lunch, dinner, or quick bites you can get anywhere - made freshly, with love just for you. From the very beginning, we have been focusing on three principles: Quality, Relatability, and Authenticity. It was obvious that if we were to survive, and ultimately thrive, we would have to deliver outstanding tastes in a timely manner on a consistent basis. Our dedication to finding the right match to each item we make, has built the trust and name all around the world! We enjoy a good challenge, and we get the most joy from seeing you enjoy everything we make. With our magic creators working their way to your hearts, the process is always fun and innovative while recognizing the rich tradition of the Indian Cuisine. Not to be outdone, ‘Telugu Foods’ is the home of South Indian delicacies with array of flavours to choose from! Whether it is a movie night with friends, family dinner or in an office break, we want ‘Telugu Foods’ to be your connection to a fun and friendly food fix, every time.

The company is ISO 22000:2018 and FSSAI certified.
